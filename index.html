Simple & Flexible

Flexr is a responsive grid for those who simply want a grid. Using only CSS, columns collapse in two ways: Expanding and Shrinking.

Check It

One of a Kind

Flexr offers a unique option for your responsive grid layout: Expanding Columns. With just one CSS class, the one, two, three, four, and six columns can expand dynamically as the screen size reduces to that of tablets and phones.

The Basic Setup

No need for containers, the Flexr Grid is made up of just rows and columns.

<nav|header|section|footer>

   <row>

      <column>

         your stuff!

      </column>

   </row>

</nav|header|section|footer>
